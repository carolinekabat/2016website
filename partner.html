How it works

The 111 participants will get 10 hours to create the best product/service. They work in teams (max five people) and can build whatever they want as long as it can stand on its own. We really want to encourage people to make something out of that idea they’ve been saving for later, rather than building something related to their job or studies. Of course, the most important part is that it’s fun and inspiring.

This is the second edition of the Gothenburg Startup Hack, successful Startup Hacks have been held in Gothenburg last year and in Stockholm several times before that.
